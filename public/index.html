How to Play

  1. Every player submits one word — anything you like.
  2. All words are revealed at once.
  3. Now submit a new word that connects the revealed words.
  4. Keep going — the goal is for everyone to submit the same word.
  5. When all words match, the game ends — fastest convergence wins!
